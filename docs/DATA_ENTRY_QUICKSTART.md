# YoMilk --- Data Entry Quick Reference Card

> Keep this on your phone or print it out.

\newpage

## Your 3 Jobs

**1. Log Deliveries** (Intake page)

- Date, Supplier, Product, Qty, Save

**2. Record Production** (Production page)

- Output Product, Output Qty, Actual Input Qty, Save

**3. Log Packouts** (Packouts page)

- Date, Product, Qty Packed, Save

---

## Search Dropdowns

- Tap the dropdown, type a few letters, tap the item
- Everything is sorted A to Z

---

## Adding Notes

- Each form has an optional **Notes** field at the bottom
- Use it to explain anything unusual: spillage, valve issues, equipment problems
- Examples: "Spillage during transfer ~20L", "Valve stuck, extra loss"
- Once saved, notes can only be changed via a Change Request

---

## Variance Guide

- **0--5%** --- Normal, nothing to do
- **5--10%** --- Slightly high, note it
- **Over 10%** --- Something may be wrong, tell your supervisor

**Always enter the real number. Never change it to match the expected value.**

---

## Made a Mistake?

1. Go to **My History**
2. Tap the **pencil icon** on the wrong value
3. Enter the correct value + reason
4. Tap **Submit**
5. Wait for Admin to approve --- the number does not change until then

---

## End-of-Day Checklist

- [ ] All deliveries logged in Intake
- [ ] All production batches recorded
- [ ] All packouts entered
- [ ] Mistakes submitted as Change Requests
- [ ] Open My History --- does everything look right?
- [ ] Log out on shared devices

---

## Do / Don't

**Do:**

- Enter the actual number
- Enter data the same day
- Double-check the product name
- Report large variances
- Use Change Requests for mistakes
- Add notes when something unusual happens
- Ask supervisor if unsure

**Don't:**

- Guess a number
- Let entries pile up
- Enter the wrong product
- Ignore big differences
- Enter zero to "fix" a mistake
- Try to delete records (only Admins can delete)
- Share your login
