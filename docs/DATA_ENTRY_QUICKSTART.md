# YoMilk — Data Entry Quick Reference Card

> Keep this on your phone or print it out.

---

## Your 3 Jobs

| Job | Page | What to do |
|-----|------|------------|
| **1. Log Deliveries** | Intake | Date → Supplier → Product → Qty → Save |
| **2. Record Production** | Production | Output Product → Output Qty → Actual Input Qty → Save |
| **3. Log Packouts** | Packouts | Date → Product → Qty Packed → Save |

---

## Search Dropdowns

- Tap the dropdown → type a few letters → tap the item
- Everything is sorted A to Z

---

## Variance Guide

| Variance | What it means | What to do |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| 0–5% | Normal | Nothing |
| 5–10% | Slightly high | Note it |
| Over 10% | Something may be wrong | Tell your supervisor |

**Always enter the real number. Never change it to match the expected value.**

---

## Made a Mistake?

1. Go to **My History**
2. Tap the **pencil icon** on the wrong value
3. Enter the correct value + reason
4. Tap **Submit**
5. Wait for Admin to approve — the number does not change until then

---

## End-of-Day Checklist

- [ ] All deliveries logged in Intake
- [ ] All production batches recorded
- [ ] All packouts entered
- [ ] Mistakes submitted as Change Requests
- [ ] Open My History — does everything look right?
- [ ] Log out on shared devices

---

## Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Enter the actual number | Guess a number |
| Enter data the same day | Let entries pile up |
| Double-check the product name | Enter the wrong product |
| Report large variances | Ignore big differences |
| Use Change Requests for mistakes | Enter zero to "fix" a mistake |
| Ask supervisor if unsure | Share your login |
